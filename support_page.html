Is my data safe with Ease Space?

Absolutely! Ease Space processes all photos locally on your device. We never upload, store, or transmit your photos to external servers. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

How does duplicate detection work?

Our advanced algorithm analyzes various factors including image content, metadata, file size, and creation dates to identify duplicate and similar photos with high accuracy.

Can I recover accidentally deleted photos?

Deleted photos are moved to your device's "Recently Deleted" album where they remain for 30 days. We strongly recommend backing up important photos before cleaning.

Why do I see multiple similar photos?

These might be burst photos, Live Photos, or photos taken in rapid succession. Our app groups similar images so you can easily select the best version to keep.

üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions:

App Won't Open Photos

Solution: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos and ensure Ease Space has "Full Access" permission.

Scanning Takes Too Long

Solution: Large photo libraries (5000+ photos) may take several minutes to scan. Ensure your device has sufficient battery and close other apps.

Not Finding All Duplicates

Solution: Our algorithm is conservative to prevent accidental deletion. Manually review the "Similar" section for additional matches.

App Crashes During Scan

Solution: Restart your device and ensure you have at least 1GB of free storage. Close other apps before scanning.
