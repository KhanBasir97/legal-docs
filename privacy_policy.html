Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ease Space ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos and Media Files

Our app accesses your device's photo library solely to identify and help you remove duplicate photos. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to external servers. All photo analysis is performed locally on your device.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect device-specific information such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Storage capacity and usage statistics
  • App performance and crash reports
  • Device identifiers for app analytics

1.3 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our app, including:

  • Features used and frequency of use
  • Number of duplicates found and removed
  • App session duration
  • In-app purchase information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our photo cleaning services
  • Analyze app performance and fix technical issues
  • Customize your user experience
  • Process premium feature purchases
  • Send important app updates and notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

Your photos remain on your device at all times. We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we collect:

  • All photo processing occurs locally on your device
  • Usage data is encrypted during transmission
  • We use secure servers with industry-standard protections
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in app functionality (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store and iTunes (for purchases)
  • Analytics providers (anonymized data only)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection practices
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
